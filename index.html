Frequently Asked Questions

Who can sell on flipkart?
Anyone selling new and genuine products is welcome. In order to start selling, you need to have: GSTIN, Cancelled Cheque, Sample Signatures.
Can I offer both products and services on Flipkart?
Currently, you can only sell products and not services on Flipkart.
Who decides the price of products?
As a seller, you will set the price of your products.
Who take care delivery of my products on Flipkart?
Our logistics partner will pick up the product from you and deliver it to the customer. All you need to keep it pack and Ready.
How many listings are required to start selling?
You are required to have a minimum of 1 listings to start selling on flipkart.
